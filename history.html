History of Solana

Solana was founded in 2017 by Anatoly Yakovenko, a former Qualcomm engineer. His goal was to solve the scalability problems faced by existing blockchains.

In 2020, Solana launched its mainnet and quickly gained attention due to its fast transaction speeds and low fees.

Over time, Solana became one of the most popular blockchains for DeFi and NFT projects, attracting developers and investors globally.

Today, Solana continues to evolve as a powerful ecosystem in the crypto space.
