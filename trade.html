How to Trade on Solana

Trading on Solana starts with creating a Solana wallet such as Phantom or Solflare. These wallets allow you to store SOL and other Solana-based tokens.

After setting up your wallet, fund it with SOL from an exchange. SOL is used to pay for transaction fees, which are extremely low on the Solana network.

You can trade tokens using decentralized exchanges like Raydium, Orca, or Jupiter. Simply connect your wallet, choose the token you want to swap, and confirm the transaction.

Solana’s high speed allows trades to complete within seconds, making it ideal for fast-paced crypto trading.
