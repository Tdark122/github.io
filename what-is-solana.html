What is Solana Blockchain?

Solana is a high-performance blockchain designed to support decentralized applications and cryptocurrencies at scale.

It uses a unique system called Proof of History, which allows the network to process thousands of transactions per second efficiently.

Solana is widely used for DeFi platforms, NFT marketplaces, Web3 games, and payment systems due to its speed and low transaction costs.

The blockchain is developer-friendly and continues to grow with strong community support worldwide.
